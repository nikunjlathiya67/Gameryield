Gameryield DAO

GAMERYIELDDAO IS A DECENTRALIZED AUTONOMOUS ORGANIZATION (DAO) GOVERNED BY ITS USERS. GAMERYIELDDAO PROTOCOL AIMS TO ACCUMULATE EARNINGS POWER IN ETHEREUM BASED GAMES AND NFT PROTOCOLS. IT ALLOWS TOKEN HOLDERS TO POOL AND OR LEND CAPITAL AS WELL AS EARN INCOME, WHILE HELPING NFT PROJECTS INCREASE FLOOR PRICES AND TOKEN VALUATIONS. GAMERYIELD(GYD) IS THE UTILITY AND YIELD TOKEN USED TO GOVERN AND INCENTIVIZE USERS IN THE ALL THE EARNING BASED DAPPS UNDER THE GAMERYIELDDAO UMBRELLA

DAPPS

TRAXPOT

TRAXPOT IS A MOOSE TRAX BASED RAFFLE GAME WHICH GENERATES ETHER REVENUE FOR GAMERYIELD HOLDERS AND ALLOWS PLAYERS TO WIN ETHER RAFFLES. USERS USE ETHER AND TRAX AT A CHANCE TO WIN A ETH JACK/TRAX - POT, WHILE STAKING GAMERYIELD TOKENS GENERATES PASSIVE ETHER YIELD FOR TOKEN HOLDERS. GAMERYIELD TOKEN HOLDERS STAKE (UNI-V2) LP TOKENS IN ORDER TO EARN ETHER AS REWARDS.

NIFTYWAREFARE

NIFTYWARFARE IS A ROBOTIC AND CYBERTRONIC "PLAY TO EARN" COMBAT MMO GAME REBRANDED AFTER 3 YEARS OF DEVELOPMENT. THE ORIGINAL IDEA DEVELOPMENT IN 2018 WAS NOVEL AND TOO EARLY AND WITHOUT THE NFT FANBASE CURRENTLY IN EXISTENCE. NIFTYWARFARE USES THE GAMERYIELD IN TOKEN AS UTILITY FEE PAYMENTS FOR ACTION. NOW WITH THE FULL REBRAND AND SMART CONTRACT REFACTORING WE ARE READY TO SEND GAMERYIELD HOLDERS TO THE MOON.

GOVERNANCE

THE GAMERYIELD TOKEN IS A GOVERNANCE TOKEN FOR PROPOSAL SIGNALS AND EXECUTION, AS WELL AS UTILITY TOKENS IN MULTIPLE HYPER DEMAND, PLAY-TO-EARN DAPPS IN THE METAVERSE. AS A GAMERYIELD TOKEN HOLDER YOU ARE THE HOUSE: YOU HELP CONTROL THE ORGANIZATION DIRECTION, BY HELPING TO DECIDE ON FEATURE PROPOSALS, DAPP PARAMETERS, VOTE ON NEW DAPP GAMES AND INITIATIVES, AND EARN EARN PASSIVE “ETHER” INCOME BY HOLDING GAMERYIELD TOKENS. GOVERNANCE TOKEN HOLDERS ALSO ARE AWARDED UNIQUE UTILITY NFT AND EARN UP TO 30% IN YIELD FARMING REWARDS.

TOKENOMICS

THE GAMERYIELD TOKEN TOKENOMIC MODEL MARRIES THE EXPLOSIVE GROWTH OF NFTS WITH THE SUPERIOR MONETIZATION CAPABILITIES OF DEFI. PARTICIPATE AND EARN REWARDS IN THE FORM OF ETHEREUM TOKENS (ETH), GAMERYIELD “GMRY” TOKENS AND NFT’S. AS A FUNDAMENTAL BUILDING BLOCK OF THE GAMERYIELD TOKEN MODEL IS BACKED BY A LIQUIDITY POOL WITH HIGH USAGE AND DEMAND VOLUME FROM MULTIPLE USE CASE DAPPS. EACH OF THE DAPPS ARE TIGHTLY INTEGRATED INTO DEX EXCHANGES SUCH UNISWAP AND USERS ARE HIGHLY INCENTIVIZED TO USE GAMERYIELD TOKEN INTERNALLY AND FOR EVERY CONTEXT WITHIN THE DAPPS. DURING DAPP USE, GAMERYIELD TOKENS ARE EITHER LOCKED UP OR BURNT, DEPENDING ON THE CONTEXT. NEW USERS OF DAPPS CAN DIRECTLY USE ETHER IN-DAPP WHICH IS IMMEDIATELY CONVERTED VIA UNISWAP TO GAMERYIELD TOKENS AND THEN BURNT, DECREASING SUPPLY AND INCREASING THE DEFLATIONARY EFFECT AND INCREASE IN GAMERYIELD TOKEN VALUATION AND PRICE.
